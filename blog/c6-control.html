"Search Inside Yourself" Chapter 5

When programming, what are some of your triggers? What causes a disproportionate emotional response?

My main trigger when programming is frustration when I can't get something to work after spending what I feel is too much time on it. The disproportionate emotions are anger, failure and the feeling of not being good enough. The flip side of that though is achieving a task and feeling elation and that I'm a genius. I think from what this chapter says is that all feelings are natural and it is how you respond to them that is the important thing.

How did you find the Siberian North Railroad exercise (Stop, Breathe, Notice, Reflect, Respond)?

I found reflecting on past incidents when I have allowed my feelings to effect my reactions very enlightening and I will definetely try in future to use the Siberian North Railroad method to regulate my response if I feel I am about to overreact. Also I will try and be more emphathatic to other people's reactions.
